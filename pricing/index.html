Frequently asked questions
Can I cancel anytime? ⌄

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time — no questions, no friction. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

What is wallet-gated access? ⌄

Wallet-gated access means membership is verified by your Ethereum wallet holding an Acceptance Pass NFT — a non-transferable credential minted on Base (an Ethereum L2). If you prefer not to use crypto, the Pro subscription gives you the same access with a regular credit card instead.

How does billing work? ⌄

Pro starts with a 14-day free trial — no charge until the trial ends. Monthly plans renew every 30 days. Annual plans are billed once per year at a discounted rate (equivalent to 10 months instead of 12). All payments are processed securely by Stripe.
